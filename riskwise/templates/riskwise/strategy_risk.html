Methodology & Assumptions

Reference Notes

What this calculates: a recommended lot-size signal derived from base lot, win rate, risk-reward ratio, and a volatility-adjusted expectancy rule.

Formula logic: the service first computes expectancy from win rate and risk-reward assumptions, then applies a capped sizing multiplier and reduces the output by a volatility divisor.

Kelly-reference boundary: win rate and risk-reward are also inputs used in Kelly-style sizing logic, but this tool is intentionally more conservative and should be treated as a capped planning heuristic rather than a Kelly allocator.

Interpretation boundary: this is a planning heuristic intended to support pre-trade review, not a statistically validated allocation model.

Limitations: it does not account for correlation between strategy assumptions, non-normal return distributions, or market-regime shifts. Use it together with your other planning surfaces.

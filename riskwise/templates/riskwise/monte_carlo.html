Methodology & Assumptions

Reference Notes

What this simulates: multiple random sequences of observed outcomes are generated by sampling with replacement from the filtered dataset. Aggregate statistics are then calculated across all runs.

Assumptions: outcomes are treated as independent and identically distributed. The model does not account for serial correlation, dynamic position sizing, or market regime shifts.

Use with caution if: the filtered dataset is small, the simulation count is low, or the trade horizon is very short. In those cases, treat the output as a planning signal rather than a robust estimate.

Methodology & Assumptions

What this simulates: Multiple random sequences of trade outcomes are generated by sampling (with replacement) from the uploaded dataset. Each sequence produces a cumulative profit curve, and aggregate statistics are calculated across all simulations.

Assumptions: Trade outcomes are treated as independent and identically distributed. The simulation does not model serial correlation, position sizing changes, or regime shifts. Filtered subsets may reduce sample size and increase variance in results.

Use with caution if: The filtered dataset contains fewer than 30 records — small samples produce unstable distribution estimates. Results should be treated as planning signals, not predictions.

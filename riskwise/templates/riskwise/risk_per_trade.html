Methodology & Assumptions

Reference Notes

What this calculates: the monetary amount at risk for a single trade, based on lot size, pip value, and stop-loss distance. The result is also expressed as a percentage of total account balance.

Assumptions: this calculation assumes a fixed pip value per lot and does not account for slippage, spread widening, or gap risk. Actual risk in volatile conditions may exceed the calculated value.

Common guideline: many capital-preservation approaches keep risk per trade within a relatively small share of account balance and review more aggressive values carefully.

Methodology & Assumptions

Reference Notes

What this calculates: the monetary risk amount and percent of account at risk for a planned trade.

Formula logic: the service multiplies lot size, pip value, and stop-loss distance to compute risk amount, then divides that by account balance to derive percent risk.

Limitations: this assumes a fixed pip value and does not account for slippage, spread widening, or execution differences.

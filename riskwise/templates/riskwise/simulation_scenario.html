Methodology & Assumptions

Reference Notes

What this compares: up to three independent Monte Carlo-style scenario runs using the same active planning dataset, but with different trade counts, ranges, or filtered periods.

Assumptions: outcomes are sampled independently within each scenario. The model does not account for serial dependence, adaptive position sizing, or structural market regime shifts.

Use with caution if: scenarios rely on very different filtered sample sizes or very short horizons. In those cases, comparison confidence is uneven and results should be treated as planning signals rather than forecasts.

Methodology & Assumptions

What this compares: Up to three independent Monte Carlo simulations run with different parameter assumptions. Each scenario samples from the uploaded dataset and produces aggregate distribution statistics (min, max, median, probability of profit).

Assumptions: Each scenario uses the same underlying dataset but may apply different trade counts, ranges, or date filters. Outcomes are sampled independently within each scenario.

Use with caution if: Scenarios use very different sample sizes — comparing a 10-trade scenario with a 500-trade scenario produces uneven confidence levels. Results are planning signals, not forecasts.

Methodology & Assumptions

What this calculates: The dollar risk (entry to stop-loss), dollar reward (entry to take-profit), and their ratio for a planned trade.

Formulas: Risk = |Entry − SL| × Lot × Pip Value  |  Reward = |TP − Entry| × Lot × Pip Value  |  R:R = Reward / Risk

Limitations: Assumes a fixed pip value and does not account for spread, slippage, or partial fills. A R:R ratio above 1.0 means reward exceeds risk; most capital preservation approaches target at least 1.5:1 or 2:1 before entry.

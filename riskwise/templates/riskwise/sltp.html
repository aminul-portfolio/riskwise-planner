Methodology & Assumptions

Reference Notes

What this calculates: risk pips, reward pips, risk amount, reward amount, and the risk-reward ratio for a planned trade.

Formula logic: the service derives pip distances from entry, stop-loss, and take-profit, then converts them into monetary values using lot size and pip value.

Interpretation boundary: a stronger risk-reward ratio does not validate the setup on its own if the absolute downside, account risk, or execution assumptions are still weak.

Limitations: this assumes a fixed pip value and does not account for spread, slippage, execution differences, or the broader portfolio context. Validate the same setup in Trade Risk Controls before approving the trade.
